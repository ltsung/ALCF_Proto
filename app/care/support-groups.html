Support Groups

ALCF Support Groups provide a safe environment for those experiencing life's challenges in the areas of moms raising young children, recovery from addiction/addiction-specific behaviors (more on recovery), coping with or supporting those with mental health issues, and healing from past wounds.

These biblically based groups are designed to foster trust, love and openness amongst participants. Groups are led by leaders who have personal experience or educational training in these areas.

Our intent is that you will experience God's love, hope, wisdom and healing through encouragement and accountability.

Financial Stewardship

The goal of the Financial Stewardship Ministry (FSM) is to point people to God's Word so they are able to apply biblical principles to managing their money and possessions.

FSM offers a 7-week, in-depth, DVD-based study using the Crown Financial Ministries Managing Our Finances God's Way, study guide. Classes are facilitated by ALCF members that are either financial professionals or have experienced personal financial breakthrough and are passionate about furthering God's Kingdom principles in the financial areas of our life.

In addition to serving brothers and sisters who have needs in realigning their spending, we explore when and how to give generously. The objectives of the study are to:

  • Discover what God's Word says about finances
  • Evaluate financial assets and debts
  • Review current spending habits
  • Create a spending plan
  • Provide encouragement and support as people work on their personal plan

Has food become more about frustration than fulfillment? Made To Crave is the missing link between a woman's desire to be healthy and the spiritual empowerment necessary to make that happen. PrayerWorks

The name of this ministry, PrayerWorks, is a statement of what we believe: God is our source and provider! While relying on His promises, we offer encouragement, prayer and spiritual support for those in job transition due to unemployed, under-employed or entrepreneurial endeavors.



PrayerWorks:
  • Offers a forum for job seekers to discuss work ethics and apply biblical principles
  • Seeks out professionals and guest speakers who will be mentors to entrepreneurs who wish to start their own business
  • Presents and shares job opportunities when made available
Ministry Participants:
  • Those who are in job transition and need prayer support
  • Prayer Warriors who come to pray on behalf of those in job transition
  • Intercessors and entrepreneurs who have successfully made a job transition

Recovery Bible Study

A coed group for those seeking healing from any addiction that has separated us from God. This group is about transformation from addiction—our compulsions and addictions that have hurt our bodies, others, and ourselves—to recovery. As we examine the Twelve Steps of recovery with a Bible-based perspective, our goal is to find a humble walk with others and with God that will lead us out of self-centeredness and closer to God’s heart.

This is not an “AA” meeting or a “12-Step” program. Participants are welcome to join at any time and, when appropriate, are encouraged to participate in a 12-step program and to attend other meetings.
