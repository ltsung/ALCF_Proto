ALCF-Hosted Events

In addition to our worship celebrations and regular ministry events that take place on the ALCF campus, we also host wedding, funerals/memorials and non-ALCF sponsored events.

Weddings

Marriage is a celebration of the love that unites a man and a woman in a relationship where “they are no longer two, but one.”—Mark 10:8

We are delighted that you are considering ALCF to host your wedding ceremony. You do not need to be a member of our church community in order to be married in our sanctuary or chapel.

Funerals/Memorials

Please accept our deepest sympathy. We are very sorry that someone close to you has passed away. Please know that your church family will do whatever we can to assist you during this difficult time. If you have not already done so, please contact one of the pastors as soon as possible. In addition, please fill out the Funerals and Memorials Request Form so we can assist you in your planning process. The events manager will be in touch with you shortly.
