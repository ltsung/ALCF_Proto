Worship Service Ministries

Worship Service Ministries (WSM) is comprised of teams that provide support for our worship services and events. It is the aim of WSM to: glorify the Lord in all we do, give support to evangelism, grow in Christ, and grow together in unity.

We invite you to join the ministries listed below in serving our church body.

Altar Prayer

Elders, pastors and lay church members who pray and intercede for the needs of others at the altar at the close of our worship services. This team also prays with people who make decisions to accept Jesus as their Lord and savior.


Contact: Pastor Tobey Hall | tobey.hall@alcf.net

Audio

A team of audio engineers who operate the audio console to record, mix and reproduce sound in our adult, youth and children services.


Contact: La Rue Ragan | audio@alcf.net

Baptism

As public acknowledgement and affirmation of a spiritual burial and resurrection, our baptism teams assists believers in participation of this sacrament of the church.


Contact: Kenneth Williams | baptism@alcf.net

CD

The CD team facilitates the production, purchase and distribution of sermon CDs after the worship service. The sermon CDs are a great way to review the message over the week or give to family or friends for encouragement. Visit the CD room located just off the main lobby to purchase a $4 sermon CD.


Contact: Frank Riley | cd@alcf.net

Communion

In remembrance of Jesus Christ's precious sacrifice for us on the cross, our Communion team serves the Lord’s supper communion at each worship service typically on the first weekend of each month.


Contact: Steve Prouty | communion@alcf.net

Greeters/Ushers

Greeters use their gift of service to provide a personal expression of Christian warmth and welcome, by providing bulletins, a smile and a welcoming atmosphere at church. Our ushers guide guests to seats, remain accessible to guests, and provide information as needed.


Contact: Larry Cooper | ushersgreeters@alcf.net

Hospitality

The Hospitality Team prepares and serves refreshments and beverages to church and ministry team members to stimulate an environment of fellowship before and after our services.


Contact: Karen Shaban | hospitality@alcf.net

Information Center

The Information Center team serves at the Information Center (IFC) before and after worship services and events to provide general information about ALCF to attendees. The team distributes equipment for the hard-of-hearing, manages the Lost and Found, and directs and assists individuals in finding locations of programs and events that meet at ALCF.


Contact: Shawneece Stevenson | infocenter@alcf.net

Medical Emergency

The Medical Emergency team serves to aid those in medical distress by providing initial medical response while awaiting arrival of (civic) emergency personnel. Team members must be certified in CPR or a trained medic, including EMT, nurse, physician or other medical professional. .


Contact: Jerrin West | emt@alcf.net

Parking

As the “first face” of ALCF, our Parking team serves to extend a warm welcome to all who come to ALCF, while directing them to appropriate parking spaces at ALCF and in the immediate area. If you are available to serve at least 60 minutes before and during the worship service, we are glad to have you join the team.


Contact: Todd Rosenbaum | parking@alcf.net

Praise & Worship

Praise & worship—including singing songs, playing instruments and dancing—is a vital part of our celebration service when we come together as believers to express our thanks to God for Who He is and for all that He has done.


Contact: Anton Nel | worship@alcf.net
