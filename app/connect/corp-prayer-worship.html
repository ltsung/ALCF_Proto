Corporate Prayer & Worship

Corporate prayer and worship are essential ingredients to a Christian’s growth and maturity within the body of Christ.

Sunday Service

Abundant Life Christian Fellowship's (ALCF) Sunday morning (10AM) service is a lively and diverse gathering of friendly and welcoming people that represent Silicon Valley’s rich melting pot. You will fit right in whether you dress casually or in your "Sunday best.”

Our 90-minute service—featuring live music and a practical, Bible-centered message that helps us connect to one another and God's truth—includes the following elements:

  • Praise & Worship
  • Announcements
  • Communion (usually first Sundays)
  • Bible-centered Message
  • Water Baptism
  • Altar Prayer

ALCF culture embraces people from ALL walks of life. No matter where you are on life’s journey, we believe that Jesus alone has the power to transform any yielded heart.

Schedule: Sundays, 11:30AM | Sanctuary

Morning Prayer

A great way to jump start your day, Morning Prayer is a specific prayer opportunity for those who want to have that special time of prayer before they begin their work day; before problems or challenges of the day begins. Using the Lord’s Prayer as a model, you will meet with others who find it spiritually energizing to come into His Presence—with praise, worship and thanksgiving—in the quietness and stillness of the dawning day.

Schedule: Tuesday–Friday | 5:30–6:30AM

Contact: Sister Geraldine Hill | prayer-visitation@alcf.net | 650.625.1500, x209

Intercessory Prayer

“I urge, then, first of all, that petitions, prayers, intercession and thanksgiving be made for all people—for kings and all those in authority, that we may live peaceful and quiet lives in all godliness and holiness.”—1 Tim 2:1 (NIV)

Intercessory Prayer offers many opportunities for believers to pray and intercede with and for others. Prayer focus includes our churches, our communities, our nation and our government. Special time is given to worship, sharing prayer requests and praying for specific needs. The meetings are always open for drop-ins or urgent requests.

Schedule: Monthly, 1st Fridays | 7:30–9PM

40 Days Of Prayer & Consecration

“They devoted themselves to the apostles’ teaching and to the fellowship, to the breaking of bread and to prayer.”—Acts 2:42

A Call To Prayer & Consecration:
The Book of Acts confirms that one of the keys to fruitful evangelism, personal renewal and overall effectiveness in ministry, is fervent prayer. When they wanted to see souls saved and lives changed, the early church prayed…and God answered! And even 2,000 years later, prayer remains the key!

Join us for our annual 40 Days of Prayer and Consecration kickoff. For more details about the prayer agenda listed below and some helpful guidelines surrounding fasting, click here for a downloadable pdf.

40 Day Prayer Agenda:

  1. Giving God praise and thanks
  2. Praying for the salvation of not-yet believers
  3. Praying for ALCF missionaries and the persecuted church
  4. Praying for personal and congregational spiritual renewal/healing
  5. Praying for those in authority
  6. Praying for believers
  7. Praising God for answered prayer
