Student Ministries

Student ministries exists to facilitate opportunities to:
  • REACH not-yet-believing students
  • CONNECT students to Christians
  • GROW students in their faith
  • CHALLENGE students to discover their unique spiritual gifts
  • Allow students to SERVE Christ in ministry

Sunday Weekend Service: 10AM
Service begins with corporate worship for both junior and senior high students, followed by small, age-specific groups led by Small Group Leaders.


Program Aspects
  • Students are warmly greeted with smiles and hugs
  • Youth-led praise and worship band
  • Ice-breaker games to build relational bridges and foster community
  • Bible-based topical lessons facilitated by caring, adult small group leaders who encourage students to grow in their faith and challenge them to apply Godâ€™s Word to real-life situations and crisis

Other Activities
  • Off-site field trips and recreational activities to foster relationships
  • Special outreach events or activities to reach other youth for Christ

Ways To Serve
  • Greeter Coordinator (organizes and trains students to serve as greeters)
  • Front Desk Coordinator (organizes and trains students to serve as a front desk representatives)
  • Small Group Leader/Aide (shepherds and leads a small group)
  • Audio/Video Coordinator (operates and manages all multimedia functions for services and special events)
  • Activities Coordinator (plans, organizes and facilitates field trips/recreational activities)
  • Special Events Coordinator (plans, organizes and facilitates outreach events/activities)
