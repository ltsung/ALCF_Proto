SAFARI Kids

SAFARI Kids (SK) ministers to the needs of families with children, from early childhood (infant) through fifth grade and children with special needs (Special Adventure).

We exist to Seek God in His Word, Abide in Him through Prayer, Find our Family in God, Adore Him through Worship, Risk everything to Serve Him and Invite everyone to Meet Him, as exemplified Acts 2:42–47.

Our mission is to love and nurture children into a lifelong relationship with Jesus.


We believe...
  • Christ has called all children to follow Him
  • Children are gifts and instruments from God and must be treated with love, care and respect
  • Children can be equipped to serve Christ and others in a dynamic and real way
  • Children deserve a safe, loving, creative and age-appropriate learning environment where they can enter into discipling relationships free of fear, pressure or prejudice
  • God has appointed parents as the primary religious instructors of their children and the church's role is to partner with parents in this task
  • Successful and effective ministry to children is a cooperative effort between the children, parents, SAFARI Kids staff, leaders and the church, reaching the children of our community with the love of Christ
  • Christians are not successes without successors

What is a Sunday service like?

SAFARI Kids begins as 10AM and runs for approximately 1.5 hours. It is filled with fun, lively and interactive music and interactive teaching. Classrooms are separated by ages: Early Childhood, Preschool through Fifth grade, and children with special needs (Special Adventure).

Parents can check children in beginning at 9:45am. Once checked in, the child is welcomed into their classroom by a leader. We start with free time and then move into our small groups that are used for building relationships, introducing the day’s story, and learning how to apply the key point. Large group time offers worship, music, and an innovative approach to the day's Bible story.

In addition to our Sunday service, SAFARI Kids offers regular Child Dedications, Walk Like Jesus (a baptism/communion class for children and parents/guardians), and an opportunity to participate in our Children’s Choir.

We welcome first-time visitors and those who are interested in serving with SK.
