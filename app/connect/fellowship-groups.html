Fellowship Groups

Whether you are single or married, a mother, or a young adult, we want to help you to live abundantly through the ministries we offer. Regardless of where you are in your spiritual journey, our church family is open to you.

Mom's Time Out

Moms’ Time Out seeks to provide moms, raising young children, a much needed "time out" in a safe and nurturing environment. Gatherings are shaped around Bible-based worship and teaching, and discussion and prayer with mentor moms, over a light brunch. In our desire to build up and strengthen families, our goal is for moms to return home refreshed and ready to prayerfully meet everyday challenges. Childcare provided.

Singles

If you have never been married, are divorced, widowed or are a single parent, then this is the community for you. Join us for our weekly Bible study and monthly Singles events. Our hope is that you will find joy, laughter, motivation, and support to purposefully live for Christ in every area of your life.

Young Adults

Whether you are married or single and in your 20s or 30s, we want to connect you to a small group community where you can meet new friends, deepen relationships and grow in your relationship with God. Join one of our small groups where we seek to be faithful followers of Christ as we study God's Word, grow with each other, support one another and share life together.

Men

We are committed to providing men with the resources to grow in Christ through small group communities. This is one of the best places to find fellowship with other men who are facing the same challenges you are in today's busy world. Join us as we seek to become the husbands, fathers, brothers and friends God intends us to be, through fellowship, discipleship, service and intimacy with God.

Women of Worth!

We seek to provide a SAFE (Spiritual, Authentic, Friendly, Encouraging) environment for all women at our Bible studies and monthly WOW! events. We want every woman to experience a life-changing relationship with Jesus Christ and to deepen her intimacy with God and others. Our hope is that as women we will be equipped to glorify God through spiritual growth and service to Him—in our families, community, and world in which we live.

Older Adults Strong In Service (OASIS)

If you are 50+ and want to be a part of a community that encourages, builds up, and fosters growth in your relationship with Christ, then this is the place for you. Join us for regular Bible study, fellowship, teaching and prayer. God continues to use us whether we are a retiree, grandparent, couple, single or widow/widower.
