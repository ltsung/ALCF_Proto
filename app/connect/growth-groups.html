Growth Groups

Content: Growth Groups (GGs) are a primary way of growing in Christ and becoming more connected at Abundant Life. Groups consist of six to 12 people who meet weekly or every other week in homes throughout the Bay Area. When we meet together, we seek to learn God’s Word, become a loving fellowship, provide spiritual care and encouragement, and look for ways to reach out to our community with the love of Jesus. It is our hope that as we do these things together, each person will experience real growth in Jesus Christ and each GG community will make a difference in the lives of many.
