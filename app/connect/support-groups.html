Fellowship Groups

ALCF Support Groups provide a safe environment for those experiencing life's challenges in the areas of moms raising young children, recovery from addiction/addiction-specific behaviors (more on recovery), coping with or supporting those with mental health issues, and healing from the past.

These biblically based groups are designed to foster trust, love and openness amongst participants. Groups are led by leaders who have personal experience or educational training in these areas.

Our intent is that you will experience God's love, hope, wisdom and healing through encouragement and accountability.

If you are seeking one-to-one biblical counseling or coaching, click here.



* Indicates pre-registration requirement. Otherwise all groups operate on a drop-in basis.

Anger Management For Men*

Moms’ Time Out seeks to provide moms, raising young children, a much needed "time out" in a safe and nurturing environment. Gatherings are shaped around Bible-based worship and teaching, and discussion and prayer with mentor moms, over a light brunch. In our desire to build up and strengthen families, our goal is for moms to return home refreshed and ready to prayerfully meet everyday challenges. Childcare provided.
