ALCF Growth Groups Forming

Growth Groups (GGs) are small group communities, of six to 12 people, who meet together regularly in homes throughout the Bay Area to: learn God’s Word, become a loving fellowship, provide spiritual care and encouragement, and look for ways to reach out to our community with the love of Jesus.
